Our goals

Thread delivers calm, practical support for individuals who find letters, forms or appointments overwhelming. We work alongside you to bring clarity, reduce stress and make tasks easier to manage. Based in Maldon and surrounding villages.

The team

Helen and Caron founded Thread with a shared belief that no one should feel alone or overwhelmed by everyday paperwork and appointments. With a calm, compassionate approach, they bring patience, understanding and practical support to people who need a helping hand. Their focus is on making things feel clearer, more manageable and far less stressful, one step at a time.

Ethos

We’ve both spent many years working alongside individuals and families who feel overwhelmed by letters, forms, appointments and complex systems. We wanted to create something calm, practical and approachable — support that works alongside existing services, not instead of them.
